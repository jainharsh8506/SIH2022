    University

    University is a governing body who administers or control all the activities and curriculum of colleges which comes under or controlled by that university. In India, there are numerous universities and under each university there are large number of colleges. A university comprises many colleges and many departments.

    StandAlone Institutions

    There are many institutions which are not affiliated to any universities but are recognized by various counsils or ministers. For the purpose of this Servey, these institution are referred as Stand-Alone institution.
